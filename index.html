Ada Lovelace

The first programmer in History.

Some curious facts about Lovelace:

  • Born in London, United Kingdom.
  • She was homeschooled by her mother and had tutors.
  • She learned science and Mathematics, becoming a mathematician.
  • Her mother was the one who insisted in her learning Sciences and Mathematics.
  • She is considered to be the first programmer because she used to take notes on how an "specific engine could transition calculation to computation."
  • There's an "Ada Lovelace Day": the second Tuesday in October.
